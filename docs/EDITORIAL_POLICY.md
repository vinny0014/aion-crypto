# Editorial Policy

1. **Sourcing** — every news item must trace to an identifiable, credible, dated source (official announcements, filings, primary documents, reputable outlets). Content without date or origin is blocked at Discovery.
2. **No fabrication** — facts, quotes and numbers are never invented. Market figures in articles must pass MARKET DATA CHECK against the live data layer.
3. **Automation with oversight** — automated drafts pass verification gates (fact, market data, image, SEO, publish) before publication; automated summaries are labeled.
4. **Separation** — news reports facts; analysis is labeled analysis; nothing is personalized financial advice, and every market page carries a disclaimer.
5. **No plagiarism** — source material informs original writing; we never republish others' articles.
6. **Corrections** — material errors are corrected with a dated note; fundamentally wrong stories are retracted with explanation (see corrections page).
